about Ratazana

Not any project...

The idea for Ratazana grew from my passion for sports and jersey collecting. I often struggled to keep track of jersey prices across different sites, which led me to create a centralized platform where fans can browse jerseys, track price changes, and receive alerts. Ratazana reflects my dedication to using technology to solve real-world problems.

features

Interactive Jersey catalog

Browse an extensive catalog of jerseys, with detailed information including prices, sizes, and availability. Easily filter through various teams and categories to find the perfect jersey.

Price tracking

Set alerts for price drops on your favorite jerseys! Our price tracking feature notifies you when prices change, ensuring you never miss a great deal.

User-friendly interface

Enjoy a seamless shopping experience with our intuitive interface. Navigate through categories, view detailed product pages, and make purchases with ease.
